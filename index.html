Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: ~ 30 minutes
  • Serves: A lot!
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 Cups
Sugar 1 Cup
Salt 1/2 Tsp
Chocolate Chips 1 Cup
Baking Soda 1 Tsp
Baking Powder 1 Tsp
Dark Molasses 1 Tbsp
Vegetable Oil 2/3 Cup
Milk 1/4 Cup
Vanilla 1 Tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farrenheit
Step 2: Combine all dry incredients in a large bowl and whisk together.
Step 3: In a smaller bowl, combine all wet ingrediants and whisk to combine.
Step 4: Slowly pour the wet ingrediants into the dry ingrediants while stirring slowly.
Step 5: Once finished pouring, mix it all together using a spoon!
Step 6: Form balls of dough according to how large you want your cookies.
Step 7: Line the cookie sheet, put the cookies on the sheet, and bake for 8-10 minutes
Step 8: Once out, let the cookies cool for 5 minutes on the pan before transfering them off!
