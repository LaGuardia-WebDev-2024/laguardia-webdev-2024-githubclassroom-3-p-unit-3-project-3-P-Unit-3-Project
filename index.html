Hot Cocoa

  • Time: 8 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients
5 cups of Whole milk, 10 tbs unsweetened cocoa powder, Light brown sugar to taste, three whole cinnamon sticks, freshly greated nutmeg, pure vanilla extract, 2 cups heavy whipping cream, white sugar to taste, and marshmallows

Step 1:

combine milk and cocoa in a large pot over the stove, whisk together until fully combined, then add cinnamon and simmer over low heat with the lid on for 5 minutes.

Step 2:

add the sugar nutmeg and vanilla, stir to combine, keep warm till serving.

Step 3:

in a cold mixing bowl, combine white sugar with vanilla and heavy cream. next, whip until stiff peaks form.

Step 4:

ladle the hot cocoa into mugs, top with whipped cream and marshmallows. then enjoy!
